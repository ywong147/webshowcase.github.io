About Us
The Yuri Restaurant Story

Yuri Restaurant was started by Chef Yuri, formerly of the prestigious three Michelin starred restaurant in Hong Kong. In 2009, the talented chef opened his first 20-seater top dim sum restaurant in Vancouver.

A year later, they would earn their own one Michelin star. The accolades for serving superb food at affordable prices soon followed. Indeed, Yuri Restaurant is often called the most affordable Michelin-starred restaurant in the world!
